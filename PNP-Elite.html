OFFICIAL PETITION

PETITION FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF SPECIALIZED WOMEN'S COURTS IN PAKISTAN

To: The Honorable Chief Justice of Pakistan
Supreme Court of Pakistan, Constitution Avenue, Islamabad

Subject: Public Interest Petition for the Establishment of Specialized Women's Courts Across Pakistan for the Protection of Women and Expeditious Justice in Cases of Gender-Based Violence

Respected Chief Justice of Pakistan,

We, the undersigned citizens of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, respectfully submit this petition in the public interest concerning the increasing incidents of violence, harassment, abduction, domestic abuse, sexual assault, and other crimes committed against women throughout the country.

Pakistan's women and girls continue to face serious threats to their safety, dignity, and fundamental rights. Reports of rape, kidnapping, harassment, domestic violence, forced marriages, workplace abuse, cyber-harassment, and other forms of violence against women are frequently reported.

While Pakistan possesses laws intended to protect women, many victims face obstacles in reporting crimes, pursuing legal remedies, and obtaining justice. Lengthy proceedings, social pressures, intimidation, stigma, and procedural difficulties often discourage victims from fully participating in the legal process.

CONSTITUTIONAL AND PUBLIC INTEREST CONCERNS

This petition is founded upon the constitutional principles of equality before law, protection of life and liberty, human dignity, equal protection of the law, and access to justice for all citizens.

NEED FOR SPECIALIZED WOMEN'S COURTS

We respectfully submit that there is an urgent need for dedicated Women's Courts throughout Pakistan with exclusive jurisdiction over matters involving rape and sexual assault, kidnapping, domestic violence, harassment, cyber-harassment, forced marriages, honour-based violence, acid attacks, and other gender-based crimes.

PROPOSED STRUCTURE

  1. Female Judicial Officers — Women's Courts should be presided over by qualified female judges possessing specialized training in dealing with gender-based crimes and victim protection.
  2. Female Legal and Support Staff — To create a supportive environment for victims, consideration may be given to appointing female prosecutors, legal aid officers, and victim-support personnel.
  3. Victim-Centered Proceedings — The courts should operate under procedures designed to protect victims from intimidation, humiliation, and unnecessary trauma during legal proceedings.
  4. Expedited Disposal of Cases — Cases involving violence against women should be heard on a priority basis to prevent prolonged delays and ensure timely justice.
  5. Psychological and Legal Support — Victims should have access to counseling services, legal assistance, and protection mechanisms where necessary.
  6. Safe Court Environment — Dedicated facilities should be established to ensure privacy, security, and dignity for women appearing before the court.

PRAYER

In light of the foregoing, the petitioners respectfully request that the Honorable Supreme Court of Pakistan consider directing the relevant federal and provincial authorities to examine and implement appropriate measures for the establishment of specialized Women's Courts across Pakistan.

Respectfully submitted,
